Makers Valley

Makers Valley is state of the art fabrication lab facility developed with the funding support of Department of Science & Technology, Department of Biotechnology and our host Institute Amal Jyothi College of Engineering. It is initiated with the vision of creating a fabrication hub amidst the greenary of Western Ghats.

Services Offered

On getting into our incubation programme following services will be offered to the incubatees

Mentoring

Quantum of experience to ignite your mind and catalyze your action.

Networking

Usage of the Internet is becoming more common due to rapid advancement of technology and power.

Promotion & Launchpad

Promote your products and launch them at the biggest audience possible in well curated events

Legal IPR & Accounting

K S C S T E has established a Patent Facilitation Center in our institite. We impart all patent related services through this.

Marketing

Market your products through government approved market places and use the key events organised by us to market your products.

Training

Training in various fields, services and sectors will be offered by us as well as our incubatees. Feel free to attend.



In addition to above mentioned services, our incubatees will be able to use all the facilities available in the Amal Jyothi College of Engineering for the development of prototypes and products.
