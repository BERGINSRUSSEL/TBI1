Technology Committee

Technology Committee was formed with an aim of fostering the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship among all departments of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering and bring out the successful ideas from them and mould them to successful startups. The committe consists of Principal, TBI Team and Faculty coordinators from all departments to the Makerhub Amal Jyothi IEDC.
